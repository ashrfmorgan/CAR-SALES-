# Brief
An interactive Car Sales Dashboard built using Power BI. The model tracks key financial KPIs including Revenue, Profit, YTD, MTD, and Expected performance, while analyzing sales trends across time periods to evaluate growth and monitor profitability.



# Project_Goal
- Analyze overall sales and financial performance interactively
- Track key financial KPIs (Revenue, Profit, YTD, MTD, EXT)
- Evaluate monthly and annual sales growth trends
- Identify performance gaps between actual and expected sales results
- Automate financial summaries and KPI calculations for improved reporting efficiency



# DataSet
- The dataset used in this project comprises structured car sales and financial performance records, including revenue, profit, and time-based sales metrics.



# How_It_Works
1. **Data Loading and Preparation**:  
   Car sales and financial data are imported into Power BI. Data cleaning and transformation are applied to ensure accuracy and consistency for reporting.

2. **KPI Calculation and Modeling**:  
   Key financial metrics are calculated and modeled:
   - **Revenue** and **Profit** are aggregated from sales data.
   - **YTD (Year-To-Date)** and **MTD (Month-To-Date)** metrics are computed for time-based performance tracking.
   - **Expected (EXT) performance** is compared against actual results to identify gaps.

3. **Data Visualization**:  
   The dashboard generates insightful visualizations:
   - **KPI Cards**: Display Revenue, Profit, YTD, MTD, and EXT metrics for at-a-glance monitoring.
   - **Sales Trend Analysis**: Line or area charts illustrating monthly and annual sales growth.
   - **Profitability Monitoring**: Visuals highlighting profit margins and performance over time.
   - **Actual vs. Expected Performance**: Comparative charts identifying gaps between projected and realized results.

4. **Output**:  
   An interactive Power BI dashboard delivering automated financial summaries and clear visual insights to support management and sales team decision-making.
