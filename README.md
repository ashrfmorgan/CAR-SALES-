Designed and developed an interactive Car Sales Dashboard using Power BI to analyze overall sales and financial performance.

Tracked key financial KPIs including Revenue, Profit, YTD (Year-To-Date), MTD (Month-To-Date), and Expected (EXT) performance.

Analyzed sales trends across time periods to evaluate monthly and annual growth.

Monitored profitability and identified performance gaps between actual and expected sales results.

Enabled data-driven decision-making by providing clear visual insights for management and sales teams.

Improved reporting efficiency by automating financial summaries and KPI calculations in Power BI.
